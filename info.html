Venue

This year CT DrupalCamp will be held at Yale University West Campus Conference Center. Located seven miles west of downtown New Haven, the West Campus Conference Center includes a large auditorium, several breakout rooms, and even a game room where fellow Drupalists can share presentations, network and even play a little fuseball...

Morning bagels and coffee will be available at 8:00 am when you register. We will also be providing a pizza lunch from Abate's of New Haven.
