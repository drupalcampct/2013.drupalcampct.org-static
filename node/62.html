SNP Technologies, Inc. ~ "SNP" for short ~ is a dynamic IT company that continuously adapts to emerging technologies and paradigms. We offer creative web development, networking services, disaster preparedness, and application integration services.

SNP has been in the Drupal space since early 2010 and is a Yale Drupal Partner and member of the Acquia Partner network.

We offer design and build solutions as well as on-going maintenance support to our Drupal customers.

SNP is located in Hamden, CT just up the road from Yale University.
