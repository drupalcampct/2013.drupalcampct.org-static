ING Drupal Journey: 2 years later

Time: 
10:40-11:30 AM
Room number: 
220
Speaker: 
Matt Wetmore
Beginner
Business and Strategy

This session is a followup to the original "ING Drupal Journey" presented two years ago at DrupalCampCT 2011. We will cover the last two years of ING using Drupal and the future.

Outline of the session:
- How ING is using Drupal
- What we've done/improved the last two years
- What we have planned for the rest of 2013 and 2014
- How will Drupal support our ING U.S. to Voya transition.
